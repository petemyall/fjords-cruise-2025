Exploring the Fjords

We woke to the peaceful surroundings of Olden, nestled at the end of a narrow fjord surrounded by dramatic peaks. The early morning light cast a gentle glow over the still waters, setting the scene for an exciting day ahead.

After breakfast at Aqua, Joanne, Luke, and I joined an excursion to the Loen Sky Lift. This remarkable cable car took us soaring from the fjord to the top of Mount Hoven in just a few minutes, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the fjord and surrounding mountains. It was a highlight of the trip, and the sheer scale of the landscape was truly awe-inspiring.
