Cruise Logbook

Day 1 – Departure from Southampton

Boarded the ship, settled into our cabin, explored the ship, and enjoyed the sailaway from Southampton in the evening.

Day 2 – At Sea

A relaxing day cruising north. We explored the ship, attended a show, and enjoyed scenic views from the deck. Formal night dinner in the main restaurant.

Day 3 – Stavanger, Norway

Arrived in Stavanger in the morning. Explored the old town (Gamle Stavanger), visited the oil museum, and strolled by the harbour.

Day 4 – Olden

Visited the beautiful village of Olden, surrounded by mountains and glaciers. Took a tour to Briksdal Glacier or explored the valley by Loen Skylift.

Day 5 – Flam

One of the highlights! Rode the famous Flåm Railway with breathtaking views of fjords and waterfalls. Browsed local shops and enjoyed Norwegian pastries.

Day 6 – Haugesund

Walked around the town centre, visited the Haraldshaugen monument, and learned about Viking history. Great views and friendly locals.

Day 7 – At Sea

Sailed south back toward the UK. Reflected on a wonderful trip, exchanged contact details with new friends, and packed up for disembarkation.

Day 8 – Arrival in Southampton

Arrived back early in the morning. Disembarked smoothly and headed home with great memories (and plenty of photos!)
