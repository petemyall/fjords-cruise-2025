Norwegian Fjords

  • Country: Norway
  • Number of Fjords: Over 1,000
  • Longest Fjord: Sognefjord (205 km)
  • Deepest Fjord: Sognefjord (1,308 m)
  • UNESCO Sites: Geirangerfjord, Nærøyfjord
  • Best Time to Visit: May to September
  • Popular Activities: Hiking, Kayaking, Scenic Cruises
