Fun Facts About P&O Iona

🏠 Home 📒 Journal 📸 Photos 🗺️ Itinerary 🏞️ Fjord Facts

1. Iona can carry up to 5,200 guests – that’s more than some small towns!

2. The ship has 15 guest decks and over 30 places to eat and drink.

3. Powered by liquefied natural gas (LNG), she’s one of the greenest cruise ships afloat.

4. The SkyDome is a glass-roofed entertainment venue with a pool that transforms into a stage.

5. There’s a whole gin distillery onboard – cheers to that!

6. Iona was named by the Duchess of Cornwall in 2021 – virtually!

7. She stretches 344 metres long – that’s about three and a half football pitches.

8. Her maiden voyage was to the Canary Islands in August 2021.
